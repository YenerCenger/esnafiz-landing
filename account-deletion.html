Delete Your Esnafiz Account & Data

If you would like to permanently delete your Esnafiz account and all related data, you can request deletion either through the app or via this page.

What will be deleted

  • Your Esnafiz account (Firebase Authentication)
  • Business data (products, stock, sales records)
  • Community data (forum topics, replies, profile info)
  • Uploaded files and notification tokens

How to request deletion

  • In the app: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • From the web: Fill in this short form: Send deletion request by email

Verification & timing

We may verify your request through your registered email address. Your account and related data will be permanently deleted within 7–30 days. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Note: You can export your data before requesting deletion by contacting us at support@esnafiz.com.
